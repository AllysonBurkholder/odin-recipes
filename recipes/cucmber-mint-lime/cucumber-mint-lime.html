Cucumber, Mint, Lime Drink

All of these things are easily grown together in areas where it's warm and the plants can access water when they grow. Use their powers together to make a refreshing and hydrating beverage!

Description

Cucumber water with a kick, pick some cocumbers you grew, pick some mint, and a lime. Just add water or if you want add some syrup to bring it together and give a litte carb boost too.

Ingredients

  • cucumbers
  • mint
  • limes
  • water
  • syrup

Steps

  1. Bring some water and syrup to a boil, infuse mint
  2. While letting cool juice cucumbers and limes
  3. Combine syrup mint infusion with cucumber lime juice
  4. Add more water as needed
