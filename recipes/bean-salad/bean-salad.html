Bean Salad

Description

A cold salad, kinda like a pasta salad but with beans and vegetables in marinade-like dressing

One great thing about this salad is you can use whatever you have on hand!

Ingredients

  • can of beans
  • cucumbers
  • tomatoes
  • vinegrette
  • herbs

Steps

  1. rinse beans
  2. cut cucumber and tomatoes into small pieces
  3. combine beans and vegetables in large bowl, toss with vinegrette
  4. season with herbs
  5. let sit for 1 hour so beans and vegetables can marinate, consider marinading in fridge so salad is cold
